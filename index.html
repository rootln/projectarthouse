Our Process

From idea to
icon. Every time.

A proven six-step framework that's taken eight creators from zero to seven figures.

01
Discovery & Strategy

We dig into your market, audience, and competitors. We identify the white space where an AI persona can dominate — before anyone else does.

02
Character Architecture

Visual identity, personality matrix, backstory, voice, and aesthetic — all codified in a bible that makes every output feel human and on-brand.

03
Content System Build

We engineer the production pipeline: hooks, formats, templates, posting cadence, and the AI workflows that keep the content fresh and algorithm-aligned.

04
Launch & Growth Sprint

Day one strategy, seed content, cross-platform activation. We push hard in the first 8 weeks to establish reach, resonance, and momentum.

05
Monetisation Engineering

Once the audience is there, we turn it into revenue. Brand partnerships, digital products, subscriptions, UGC — every stream designed for the persona.

06
Scale & Optimise

We continuously analyse, iterate, and expand. New platforms, new formats, new revenue lines. The system compounds — so does the income.
